Studies

B.Sc. Software Engineering

Between 2011 and 2014 I finished my bachelor at Chalmers University of Technology. This programme mainly focused on Java development through many different group projects. This ment that we developed both programming skills, project management skills and how to perform a software development project with Scrum.

During this time I was also involved in the Student Union in different way. Between 2013 and 2014 I was Chairman of the Software Engineering Student Division.

M.Sc. Entrepreneurship & Business Design

This master programme is also know as Chalmers School of Entrepreneurship and is well know to be one of the best educations in entrepreneurship in both Sweden and Europe. During the first year the courses focus on teaching the different aspects of entrepreneurship and startup, and during the second year you start your own startup, which also is your master thesis project. During this year you work together with the incubator Encubator, one of the ten best university incubators in the world.
